Night shift fat loss tips

🛏
Blackout blinds or a sleep mask are non-negotiable — daylight halves melatonin production and destroys sleep quality.
☕
No caffeine after midnight if sleeping at 7am. Caffeine has a 6-hour half-life.
📵
Phone on flight mode. Every notification spikes cortisol during light sleep — this directly stalls fat loss.
😴
Poor sleep raises ghrelin (hunger hormone) and suppresses leptin (fullness hormone). No amount of willpower beats disrupted sleep hormones consistently.
🎰
Vending machine at 3am — hunger at that hour is 80% boredom + cortisol. Drink 500ml water, wait 10 min.
🌅
Big meal when you get home (7am) — insulin spikes just before sleep = maximum fat storage. If you need anything, keep it under 200 kcal, protein only.
⚡
Energy drinks during shift — the sugar crash at 4am is worse than the tiredness. Stick to black coffee or green tea before 1am, then water only.
🏋
"I'll gym after the shift" — this never happens. Post-shift training damages results. Pre-shift or day-off only, non-negotiable.
☀
Vitamin D3 (2,000–4,000 IU) — night workers get near zero sunlight. Deficiency tanks energy and fat metabolism. Take with 3pm "breakfast".
💤
Magnesium glycinate (300mg) — take at shift end (6am). Improves sleep quality, reduces cortisol, supports muscle recovery.
🥛
Casein protein before sleep (7am) — digests slowly over 8hrs, keeping you in an anabolic state through the sleep window.
🐟
Omega-3 (1–2g EPA+DHA) — reduces the chronic low-grade inflammation that night shift causes. Take with biggest meal.
